360 Squash

  • Tags: 
  • Game, 
  • VR, 
  • Unity

360 Squash is a fast-paced squash inspired VR game me and some fellow students created during a 24h game jam on campus of Darmstadt University of Applied Sciences.

My tasks:

  • Coding one third of the game logic especially the ball movement.
  • Designing the art style and modeling the 3D models.
  • Creating the Particle effects.
  • Designing the game mechanics and the game loop.
  • Team management and coordination.
  • Integration of the different parts of the game.
